0.4.0 (2010-11-04)
==================

  [kdeldycke]

  [kdeldycke]

  [kdeldycke]

- Allow custom components to have no owner.
  [kdeldycke]

  [kdeldycke]

- Add initial support for multiple repositories.
  [kdeldycke]
